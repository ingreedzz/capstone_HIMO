Over 70% of young adults experience high stress, without even recognizing it. Unchecked stress can lead to burnout, anxiety, and depression. That’s why tracking your emotional state is the first step towards better mental health.

VISION & MISSION

HiMO aims to make emotional self-awareness a natural part of daily life — by empowering individuals to understand and manage their mental well-being through empathetic, intelligent technology.

HiMO is committed to helping people track, visualize, and understand their stress levels using AI-powered tools. We aim to provide a safe, judgment-free space where users can reflect on their emotions and gain insights through AI.
